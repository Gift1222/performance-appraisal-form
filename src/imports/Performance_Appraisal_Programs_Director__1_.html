1. APPRAISAL OVERVIEW

This 360-degree performance appraisal evaluates the Programs Director across five core dimensions derived from the role’s scope of responsibilities. It places strong emphasis on Program Management, Fundraising, and Strategic Leadership, recognizing their critical role in driving the success of Emerge Livelihoods’ Strategy 2024–2029.

3. PERFORMANCE DIMENSIONS
A. PROGRAM PLANNING, MANAGEMENT AND IMPLEMENTATION

Objective: To ensure the effective leadership, strategic alignment and quality implementation of all programs under Emerge Livelihoods.

Key Result AreaDescriptionRatingComments
Strategic LeadershipProvides leadership in developing strategy and budgets aligned with the Emerge Livelihoods Strategic Plan 2024–2029.
Operational PlanningSupports the Managing Director in developing strategic and operational plans based on strategic guidelines.
Program AlignmentReviews and ensures alignment of program priorities, strategies, and plans with core organisational strategy and the localization strategy.
Implementation QualityEnsures quality and effective implementation of all projects, demonstrating value for money and return on investment.
Knowledge ManagementEnsures all learning and knowledge is documented effectively by the programs team.
B. FUNDRAISING AND DONOR RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT

Objective: To drive resource mobilization by developing and managing relationships with funding partners and creating high-quality, participatory grant proposals.

Key Result AreaDescriptionRatingComments
Donor Relationship ManagementSupports the development and management of strong relationships with local and international funding partners.
Proposal DevelopmentLeads the development of program ideas and concepts into quality grant proposals using a participatory community approach.
Resource MobilizationCollaborates with the Partnership and Resource Development Department to respond to diverse funding opportunities.
C. PROGRAM REVIEWS, MONITORING, EVALUATION AND LEARNING

Objective: To ensure robust monitoring, evaluation, accountability, and learning systems are in place to track progress, ensure compliance, and promote evidence-based decision-making.

Key Result AreaDescriptionRatingComments
Monitoring AND EvaluationEnsures close monitoring of all program activities to track information flow and the systematic documentation of achievements and lessons learned.
ReportingEnsures timely and quality submission of quarterly, bi-annual, and annual program reports according to desired standards.
ComplianceEnsures adequate compliance with approved program/project plans and organizational policies.
Evidence-Based LearningPromotes evidence-based learning, documents lessons, and represents Emerge Livelihoods in knowledge-sharing platforms.
D. PARTNERSHIPS AND NETWORK

Objective: To strengthen, manage, and leverage networks and partnerships to enhance the impact and reach of Emerge Livelihoods’ work.

Key Result AreaDescriptionRatingComments
Network StrengtheningSupports Program Managers in strengthening networks and rapport with key strategic networks and consortiums.
RepresentationRepresents Emerge Livelihoods in network meetings, events, and forums at national and international levels.
Partnership ManagementEnsures effective partnerships at all levels per organizational standards and coordinates partnership reviews.
CollaborationEnsures effective information flow with other key stakeholders, including subsidiary teams (Emerge Fund, Emerge Ventures) and academia.
E. FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT

Objective: To manage the department’s financial resources effectively and efficiently, ensuring alignment with budgets and policies.

Key Result AreaDescriptionRatingComments
Budgeting AND PlanningDevelops, monitors, reviews, and updates the programs department activity and work plan to ensure cost-effective attainment of objectives.
Expenditure ManagementManages the expenditure and disbursement of resources allocated to the program department.
Financial ReviewReviews program concepts and financial requests to ensure alignment with financial management policies.
Monitoring AND ReportingMonitors and advises on program spending and variance reports in accordance with organizational procedures.
F. HUMAN RESOURCE MANAGEMENT

Objective: To lead, manage, and develop the programs team to achieve its goals in a motivating and empowering environment.

Key Result AreaDescriptionRatingComments
Team Leadership and ManagementLeads and manages the program team to achieve mandated goals according to annual operational plans.
Staff DevelopmentProvides induction, training, coaching, and mentoring; identifies training needs and development opportunities in collaboration with People and Culture.
Performance ManagementAssigns performance goals, conducts comprehensive performance appraisals and provides feedback to team members.
Work EnvironmentEnsures an empowering and motivating work environment incorporating team building and core values.
Recruitment and OnboardingParticipates in the recruitment of staff and review of job descriptions under the supervision of the People and Culture department.
G. POLICY MANAGEMENT AND COMPLIANCE

Objective: To ensure adherence to all organizational policies and procedures and to contribute to their development and review.

Key Result AreaDescriptionRatingComments
Policy DevelopmentLeads the development and implementation of national-specific guidelines for partnership management.
Policy ComplianceImplements all necessary policies and procedures and ensures they are adhered to by the programs team.
Advisory RoleParticipates in the review and update of policies and advises the Senior Management Team on implications of new policies.
